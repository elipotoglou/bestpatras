About the Course

Organizer: Have you ever wondered why you like 'I want it that way' by Backstreet Boys so much?

In this 10 day Course, we are going to dive into the science behind some major audio technology, as well as human perception and emotional response to music.

Have you ever wondered how your noise cancellation headphones work? Audio technology has dominated modern days direction towards the music and tech industry. Sound is traveling in mechanical waves which enter the human ear and cause vibrations that are enabling hearing. Music as a sound, is evoking some powerful emotional responses, shaping personal preferences and a very strong connection to TikTok sounds.

Patras

Patras is located in the northern Peloponneseand it's the regional capital of Western Greece. According to mythology, its name comes from Patreus, who was the founder of the city. Patras has a big port which is a nodal point for trade and communication with Italy and the rest of Western Europe.

Nearby, Rio-Antirrio Bridge is the world's longest multi-span cable-stayed bridge which is also widely considered to be an engineering masterpiece.

Some Fun Facts!

Patras Carnival

It is the largest carnival in Greece and like most carnival events in the Mediterranean and the Balkans was associated with ancient pagan events and rituals, such as those in honor of the god Dionysus(god of wine and feasting)!

Rio-Antirio Bridge

It is a cable-stayed bridge with a length of 2880 meters and it has been calculated that it can withstand a possible collision of a tanker with a displacement of 180,000 tons!

Name

The name of the city is based on mythology and comes from Patreas, the mythical settler of the city, who came as head of settlers in the area of Aroi and expelled the Ionian inhabitants. He gave the settlement the name Patrai.

Location

Patras is located on the north coast of the Peloponnese. It extends from the western ends of Mount Panacheikos to the sea of Patras. There are both sea and mountains for all tastes!
